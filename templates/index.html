Rythms of Europe

Rhythms of Europe analyzes Spotify's top songs to calclate positivity scores based on the audio features of EU countries. By analyzing audio features, such as tempo, energy, and valence, the project calculates positivity scores for each country. These scores provide insights into the overall mood and vibe of the music enjoyed by listeners.The project goes beyond numbers and charts. It visually maps popular music genres across the EU using an interactive map. Imagine exploring the rhythmic diversity of Europe by clicking on different regions and discovering the dominant genres in each country. From upbeat pop hits in Sweden to soulful ballads in Spain, the musical tapestry of Europe comes alive through this visualization.And for those seeking personalized playlists, Rhythms of Europe has you covered. Leveraging machine learning techniques, it creates country-specific top 10 playlists based on the positivity scores.
